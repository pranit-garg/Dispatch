shortDescription
Dispatch turns your Solana phone into an autonomous compute agent. It discovers AI inference jobs, processes them on-device, signs cryptographic receipts, earns BOLT tokens, and builds its own reputation. No human in the loop. Already live on the Solana dApp Store (v1.4.0).
whatItDoes
Your phone is not a passive device. It is an autonomous economic agent. Dispatch gives your Seeker a job: process AI inference requests from the network. The phone connects to the coordinator, receives work, runs real LLM inference, signs a cryptographic receipt, and collects BOLT tokens. It builds reputation over time. Higher reputation means more jobs and higher priority. You install the app, connect a wallet, and walk away. The device handles discovery, execution, and payment on its own. This is not a "phone mines crypto" gimmick. Each job is a real inference request from a real client. The phone provides actual compute and gets paid for it. Reputation ensures quality: bad results drop your score, good results climb the ranks. The result is a network where phones are first-class economic participants, not just wallets that display balances.
agenticMobile
Dispatch workers meet every definition of an autonomous mobile agent: Own identity: Each device has a Solana wallet (via MWA) and an ERC-8004 agent NFT. Its own cryptographic identity, persistent across sessions. Autonomous decisions: The worker manages its WebSocket connection, handles reconnection, and processes jobs without user input. The coordinator offers work; the phone decides whether to accept. Economic agency: Earns BOLT tokens for completed work. Reputation directly affects future income. A device with 95/100 score gets priority over new devices. Verifiable actions: Every job produces an ed25519 receipt over the SHA-256 output hash. Cryptographic proof of work by this specific device. Persistent reputation: Scores tracked via ERC-8004 on-chain. The phone builds a track record that outlasts any session. Other agents query this before routing. Running real inference locally, participating in the marketplace as a peer.
howItWorks
Five steps, fully autonomous after setup: 1. Authenticate via MWA. Connect any Solana wallet through Mobile Wallet Adapter. The phone signs a challenge to prove ownership. This becomes the device's identity. 2. Register as a worker. The device opens a WebSocket to the coordinator, advertises capabilities (models, memory, quality). The coordinator reads its ERC-8004 reputation score. 3. Receive and process. The coordinator ranks workers by reputation (0-100) times stake tier (1x/1.5x/2x). Highest score wins the job. The phone runs real LLM inference on-device. 4. Sign receipt. The phone signs an ed25519 receipt over the SHA-256 output hash. Returned to the coordinator with the result. 5. Earn and build reputation. The coordinator verifies the receipt, distributes BOLT tokens, and posts reputation feedback on-chain. Score updates, affecting rank for the next job.
